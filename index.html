01About//

I'm a Mechatronic Systems Engineering student at Western University (Class of 2028), a two-time Dean's List recipient, and a builder at heart. I like problems that live at the intersection of mechanical design, electronics, and code — where a part has to be modeled, printed, wired, and programmed before it does anything useful.

That's taken me from designing a printer enclosure for a university research group, to building a budget-constrained eraser-sorting robot around an ESP32, to the factory floor at Umicore — where I currently work as a maintenance intern and shipped a new automatic inventory tracking system from proposal to tested implementation.
